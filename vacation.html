Welcome to Valencia

A hidden gem on Spain's west coast

Things about Valencia to love and visit

I went to Spain in 2018 and here are some of the items I remember liking

  • Valencia has an old market that opens every morning. Each morning you can walk to it and grab your groceries or a snack.
  • Valencia has a park that cuts the city in half. You can bike on it and get anywhere in the heart of the city.
  • Paella, many many types of paella.
  • A beautiful art museum that's been in many movies!
